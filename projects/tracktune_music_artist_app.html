TrackTune is a mobile application built with React Native that empowers music artists to plan, track, and manage their creative journey—from the first spark of a song idea to the final release on platforms like Spotify and YouTube. The app allows artists to capture ideas, upload lyrics, audio, and related media, generate content using AI, and provides a dynamic timeline to ensure every milestone is met before release deadlines.

Business Impact:

TrackTune simplifies the complex journey of creating and releasing music, helping artists stay on schedule and organized. With AI-powered generation tools and insight dashboards, artists can make informed creative and release decisions. The app enhances productivity, improves content quality, and supports timely professional releases across major streaming platforms.

Additional Information:

The app is designed for solo artists, bands, and music producers alike, with a focus on intuitive UI/UX and seamless asset management. AI tools enable creative ideation and iteration, while data insights help artists gauge and optimize their musical impact. Built with React Native for cross-platform efficiency and scalability.

Features:

  • Scribble and save song ideas, notes, and rough drafts.
  • Upload and manage assets such as lyrics, images, audio files, and reference material.
  • Generate or regenerate lyrics, audio samples, and album images using AI.
  • Visual timeline to track progress and plan milestones for album or single releases.
  • Track insights and performance metrics for albums and individual tracks.
  • Integrates reminders for key tasks like recording, mastering, artwork submission, and publishing.
  • Export-ready formats and packaging for distribution platforms like Spotify and YouTube.
  • Supports both Android and iOS platforms using React Native with Redux state management.
  • Secure local and cloud asset storage and retrieval.
